Anime

"Kimi no Na wa" is a Japanese animated film about two high school students who swap bodies. They develop a connection and try to find each other. The movie explores themes of love, fate, and identity. It is known for its stunning animation and captivating story.

"Spirited Away" is a Japanese animated film about a girl named Chihiro who gets trapped in a magical world and works at a bathhouse for spirits. She tries to save her parents and find her way back home. The movie is known for its beautiful animation and imaginative storytelling. It has received critical acclaim and is considered one of the greatest animated films ever made.
